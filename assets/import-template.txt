ManAgeX — Bulk Owner / Tenant Import Template
==============================================

INSTRUCTIONS
------------
1. Fill in the columns below. Do not change the column headers.
2. For sub-tenants: fill Unit No, Sub-tenant Name, Relationship, and leave Owner fields blank.
3. Accepted Relationship values: Owner | Spouse | Child | Domestic Worker | Sub-tenant (Renting) | Other
4. For Occupancy Type: Owner-occupied | Renting out | Owner overseas
5. Upload this file via ManAgeX → Owners & Tenants → Import Excel
6. ManAgeX will email approval requests for each new record.

COLUMNS (paste into Excel row 1)
---------------------------------
Unit No | Owner Name | Owner NRIC/FIN | Owner Email | Owner Mobile | Occupancy Type | Sub-tenant Name | Sub-tenant NRIC/FIN | Sub-tenant Email | Sub-tenant Mobile | Relationship | Monthly Fee (SGD) | Notes
